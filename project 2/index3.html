Gfriend (여자친구) is a Kpop group under the entertainment company, Source Music. They consist on 6 members: Sowon, Yerin, Eunha, Yuju, Sinb, and Umji. They debuted on January 15, 2015 witht their album Season of Glass. They gained popularity from the general public despite coming from a small company. Their most famous song, Rough, gained them the title of the first and only artist to achieve a double-consecutive grandslam with one single song on music programs.

Gfriend has a total of 36 albums including 3 studio albums, 11 mini albums, 13 singles, 1 japanese studio album, 1 japanese compilation album, and 7 japanese singles. They also have 3 lightsticks, v1 which is a glass marble filled with water. This lightstick is reminiscent of their debut song, Glass bead. Their second lightstick, v2, has a purple and blue logo of the Gfriend logo. Their third lightstick, v3, is a Japanese limited edition lightstick with the colors of gold and white.

The group disbanded on May 22, 2021. However, the members have each signed onto new companies and have debuted with a recent comeback from a trio formed by former members.

Viviz

Viviz (비비지) is a South Korean Kpop group consisting of three members: Eunha, Sinb, and Umji. They are under the entertainment company, Big Planet Made. They debuted on February 9, 2022 with their first album Beam of Prism. The group's name "bi-bi-ji", is a word play( in Korean) to the names of the three members: Eunha (Jung Eun-bi), SinB (Hwang Eun-bi) and Umji.
